What is Adapting Academics?

Adapting Academics is an online math education resource made by a single developer who tutors math. However, given the small size of Adapting Academics small oversights may lead to some errors or unclear explainations. If you have any questions about the resources from Adapting Academics, concerns regarding issues, or suggestions for improvements please feel free to complete the form on this page. By filling out the form you help the developer improve the page and help Adapting Academics fulfill its goal of making learning more adaptable for everyone.
